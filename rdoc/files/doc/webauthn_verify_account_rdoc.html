Documentation for WebAuthn Verify Account Feature¶ ↑

The webauthn_verify_account feature implements setting up an WebAuthn authenticator during the account verification process, and making such setup a requirement for account verification. By default, it disables asking for a password during account creation and verification, allowing for completely passwordless designs, where the only authentication option is WebAuthn. It depends on the verify_account and webauthn features.
