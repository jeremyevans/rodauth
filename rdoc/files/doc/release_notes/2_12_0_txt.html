New Features¶ ↑

  • The following configuration methods have been added to the active_sessions feature:

    • active_sessions_insert_hash

    • active_sessions_key

    • active_sessions_update_hash

    • update_current_session?

    These methods allow you to control what gets inserted and updated into the active_sessions_table, and to control whether to perform updates.

Other Improvements¶ ↑

  • A typo was fixed in the default unlock account email.
